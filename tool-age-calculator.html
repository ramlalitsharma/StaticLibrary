What is an Age Calculator?

An Age Calculator is an online tool that helps you find your exact age in years, months, and days from your date of birth. It’s useful for birthdays, official forms, and personal records.

How to Use This Age Calculator

  1. Enter your date of birth.
  2. Click Calculate Age to see your result instantly.

Example Use Cases
