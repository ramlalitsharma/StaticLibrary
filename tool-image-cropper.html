What is the Image Cropper Tool?

This tool crops images to your chosen area. Great for web, social media, and sharing.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Select an image file.
  2. Enter the X, Y, width, and height for the crop area.
  3. Click Crop and download the cropped image from the output area.

Benefits & Features

  • Modern UI: Clean, glassmorphic design with icons.
  • Fast & Efficient: Crops images in your browser.
  • Free & Unlimited: No registration or fees required.

Example Use Cases

  • Crop images for profile pictures or thumbnails.
  • Prepare images for social media or web.
  • Adjust image area for presentations or documents.
