What is a BMI Calculator?

A BMI Calculator is an online tool that helps you determine your Body Mass Index, a key indicator of healthy body weight. It’s essential for fitness, health tracking, and medical assessments.

How to Use This BMI Calculator

  1. Enter your height in centimeters and weight in kilograms.
  2. Click Calculate BMI to see your result instantly.
  3. Check your BMI category (Underweight, Normal, Overweight, Obese).

Benefits & Features

  • Ultra-Modern UI: Beautiful, glassmorphic design with icons and gradients.
  • Fast & Accurate: Instant BMI calculation and category.
  • Mobile Friendly: Works on all devices.
  • Free & Unlimited: No registration or fees required.

Example Use Cases

  • Track your fitness and health progress.
  • Assess healthy weight for adults and teens.
  • Use in gyms, clinics, and schools.
