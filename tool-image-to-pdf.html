What is the Image to PDF Tool?

This tool converts images to PDF files instantly. Great for sharing, archiving, and printing.

How to Use This Tool

  1. Select an image file.
  2. Click Convert and download the PDF from the output area.

Benefits & Features

  • Modern UI: Clean, glassmorphic design with icons.
  • Fast & Efficient: Converts images in your browser.
  • Free & Unlimited: No registration or fees required.

Example Use Cases

  • Convert scanned documents to PDF.
  • Archive images in a single file.
  • Prepare images for printing or sharing.
