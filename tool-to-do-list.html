    What is the To-Do List Tool?

    This tool helps you organize your daily tasks and boost productivity. Add, check off, and delete tasks easily.

    How to Use This Tool

    1. Type a new task and click Add.
    2. Check off tasks as you complete them.
    3. Delete tasks you no longer need.

    Benefits & Features

    • Modern UI: Clean, glassmorphic design with icons.
    • Persistent: Your list is saved in your browser.
    • Free & Unlimited: No registration or fees required.

    Example Use Cases

    • Plan your day or week.
    • Track project tasks.
    • Organize shopping or packing lists.
